A Little About me

I'm a software engineer with experience in many areas of computer science, including computer graphics and scientific visualization, web development, digital signal processing, and parallel processing. I'm a problem solver and a quick learner who can pick up new skills easily.

I've worked as a teaching assistant, instructing first-year computer science students in learning the fundamental aspects of functional and object-oriented programming in Python. I've also worked as a research assistant, working with applying in situ computer graphics techniques to exascale scientific visualizations. I've also design audio plug-ins using JUCE in my freetime for use in my own music.

I am a multi-instrumentalist, singer-songwriter, producer, and a cappella arranger. I've played guitar since 2010 and have sung extensively, including in choirs and a cappella groups, throughout my life, most recently acting as the Music Director of University of Oregon's On the Rocks, and a singer in the wedding band, Generations. I also make audio plugins.

I'm currently working on my debut album, "bloom". My first single "CHANGED" is streaming now on all major platforms. Contact me if you need songwriting, production, mixing, or a cappella arranging services.
